# Artspectiv Studio — domain language

Ubiquitous product terms for agents and humans. **Not** Decision Log, roadmap, or technical harness detail — see `AGENTS.md` and `PROGRAM.md`. **Full commercial detail:** `docs/commercial-strategy.md`.

## Business

**Artspectiv Studio** — premium photo, reel, and social content studio based in **New Barnet**, serving **North London** and nearby areas.

**Core promise:** Monthly visual content for local businesses whose online presence should match the quality of the real-life experience.

**Audience:** Cafés, brunch spots, restaurants, bars, salons, nail studios, beauty clinics, and local lifestyle brands.

## Commercial model

- **Productised monthly retainers** — not open-ended freelance or “social media help”.
- **Side-hustle-safe capacity:** max two paid shoots/month when demand spikes; weekly rhythm = shoot, edit, admin, buffer.
- **Local referral gravity:** Barnet independents are a connected network; one nearby client beats a generic portfolio.

## Strategy pillars

1. **Proof before packages** — written Rita testimonial before heavy outreach.
2. **Growth is core** — Monthly Content Bank is the main product; Starter proves fit; Campaign for launches.
3. **Win two first** — one hospitality + one beauty/salon, not six clients at once.
4. **Verify before quoting** — IG followers, review counts, and awards go stale; recheck before pitches.
